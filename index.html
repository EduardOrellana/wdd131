Guatemala

Guatemala, a Central American country bordered by Mexico to the north and west, Belize to the northeast, Honduras
to the east, and El Salvador to the southeast, is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse landscapes,
and Mayan history. With a population of over 17 million people, Guatemala boasts a blend of indigenous
traditions, colonial architecture, and natural wonders such as Lake Atitlán and the ancient Mayan ruins of
Tikal. Despite its natural beauty and cultural significance, Guatemala faces social and economic challenges,
including poverty, political instability, and issues related to crime and corruption. Yet, efforts towards
development, tourism promotion, and preservation of its cultural heritage continue to shape Guatemala's
identity and future trajectory.
